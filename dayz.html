Server FAQs.

Below are a few commonly asked questions we believe can help aid you in joining our community!

What is DayZ?
DayZ is a hardcore open-world survival game with only one rule: stay alive, no matter what. But with a myriad of threats lurking around every corner, that might be easier said than done...
Can I join this DayZ Server on Playstation / Xbox?
Unfortunately, our server is only accessible to PC Players due to restrictions on the game itself by the developers.
Any Beginner Tips?
1. DEATH; You’re going to die. Get used to it. You’re going to die 1,001 different ways. Some will make sense, some won’t, some will be from no fault of your own. Most will be because of your own stupidity. The game has issues, if you don’t learn those issues and plan for them, you’re the idiot. 2. DESYNC; Desync is the biggest issue you will face in this game. Get used to it. Plan for it. Keep your eyes open for it. As soon as you can’t pick something up, can’t eat something, can’t fix something, exit the game immediately and rejoin the server. Save yourself the tears and just exit and relog at the first sign of desync. It’s not worth getting the drop on someone, sneaking up to them and trying to blast them in the back with your shotgun only for them to turn around and see some idiot staring at him holding a rag. 3. FOOD; Find food, food is the most important thing to find at the beginning of a new life. You’ll die of starvation a lot as food is scarce, but it is also everywhere.
What should I do if I encounter a rulebreaker in the server?
Most servers have moderators or administrators who enforce the rules. If you encounter a rulebreaker, you can report them to the server staff using the appropriate channels, such as in-game reporting systems (in our server, you can type /report in chat) or through dedicated channels within the discord server (of forums dependent on the server).
Is there an age requirement? And if so, am I too young to join?
Depending on the server, there may be an age requirement. Here in DrillUK, we do not disallow access to our server at any age, but recommend anyone 13 or younger have a responsible sibling or adult nearby due to the violence and heavy language that sometimes occurs in the server during interactions.
How do I join the server?
Firstly, ensure you have a legal version of DayZ installed on your computer. Next install the DZSALauncher here: https://dayzsalauncher.com/ and install the application. Once you've set it up, you will be able to search for the server by clicking "Browse Servers" and searching "Drill-UK". You will see our server pop up.
