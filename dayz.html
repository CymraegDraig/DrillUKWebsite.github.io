Server FAQs.

Below are a few commonly asked questions we believe can help aid you in joining our community!

What is GTA V RP?
GTA RP stands for Grand Theft Auto Roleplay. It's a multiplayer modification for the Grand Theft Auto series that allows players to immerse themselves in various role-playing scenarios within the game world. Players create their own characters and interact with others in a virtual city, assuming different roles such as police officers, criminals, business owners, and civilians. The focus is on creating realistic and engaging role-playing experiences, with players adhering to certain rules and guidelines to maintain immersion and enhance the overall gameplay experience.
Can I play GTA V RP / FiveM on Playstation / Xbox?
Unfortunately, FiveM is only accessible to PC Players. However, if you are interested smaller communities do exist where they RP on console whilst on GTA V Online, and not on the FiveM modification itself.
How do I find a job in GTA RP?
Many GTA RP servers offer various job opportunities within their virtual city, ranging from legal professions like police officers, paramedics, and other businesses to illegal activities like drug dealing, smuggling, and organized crime. Typically, you can inquire about available jobs in-game or through meeting people in character.
What should I do if I encounter a rulebreaker in GTA RP?
Most servers have moderators or administrators who enforce the rules. If you encounter a rulebreaker, you can report them to the server staff using the appropriate channels, such as in-game reporting systems (in our server, DrillUK, you can type /report in chat) or through dedicated channels within the discord server (of forums dependent on the server).
How do I make money in GTA RP?
Earning money in GTA RP varies depending on the server and your chosen role. Legal jobs usually involve completing tasks or missions, while illegal activities might include selling contraband, robbing stores, or participating in criminal enterprises.
Is there an age requirement? And if so, am I too young to join?
Depending on the server, there may be an age requirement. Here in DrillUK, we do not disallow access to our server at any age, but recommend anyone 13 or younger have a responsible sibling or adult nearby due to the violence and heavy language that sometimes occurs in the server during RP scenes.
How do I join the server?
Firstly, ensure you have a legal version of GTA V installed on your computer. Next you must go to https://fivem.net/ and install the application. Once you've set it up, you will be able to search for the server by clicking "Browse Servers" and searching "Drill-UK". You will see our server pop up.
